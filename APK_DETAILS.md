## 📲 Installation Methods

### Method 1: Direct Download (Easiest)

1. Open this page on your **Android phone's browser**
2. Go to [Releases](https://github.com/Msumankumar05/My-Tasks-app/releases/latest)
3. Tap the **`.apk` file** to download
4. Open the downloaded file and tap **"Install"**
5. Done! ✅

### Method 2: Transfer via USB

1. Download the APK on your **computer**
2. Connect your Android phone via **USB cable**
3. Copy the APK to your phone's **Downloads** folder
4. Open your phone's **File Manager**
5. Navigate to Downloads and tap the APK
6. Tap **"Install"** → Done! ✅

### Method 3: Transfer via Email/Cloud

1. Download the APK on your computer
2. Email it to yourself or upload to **Google Drive/Dropbox**
3. Open the email/cloud app on your phone
4. Download and install the APK

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **First-time installation:** You may need to enable **"Install from Unknown Sources"**
> - Go to **Settings → Security → Unknown Sources** → Enable
> - On Android 8+: **Settings → Apps → Browser → Install Unknown Apps** → Allow

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|:--------|:---------|
| "Parse Error" during install | Check that your Android version is **7.0 or higher** |
| "App Not Installed" error | Free up at least **50 MB** of storage, or uninstall any older version first |
| App crashes on launch | Restart your device, then reinstall the APK |
| Notifications not appearing | Grant notification permissions in **Settings → Apps → My Tasks → Notifications** |
| Reminders not firing | Disable battery optimization for the app |
